Venue

Resort setting

Lincolnshire Marriott Resort sits on 175 acres in Chicago’s North Shore suburbs and includes event venues, restaurants, golf, spa amenities, and the Marriott Theatre on property.

Where to go

  • Lincolnshire Marriott Resort, 10 Marriott Drive, Lincolnshire, Illinois 60069.
  • Complimentary on-site parking is available for guests.
  • Check-in begins at 4:00 pm and check-out is at 11:00 am for hotel stays.

What’s on site

  • Four restaurants and bars, an on-site Starbucks, a spa, indoor and outdoor pools, and a fitness center.
  • Crane’s Landing Golf Club is on property, and the resort also has bicycle rental and direct access to nearby outdoor recreation.
  • Marriott Theatre is part of the resort experience for guests staying the weekend.

Travel

Getting there

For most out-of-town guests, O’Hare is the closest airport. The resort does not provide shuttle service, so plan for a rideshare, taxi, rental car, or pre-arranged car service.

By air

  • Nearest airport: Chicago O’Hare International Airport (ORD).
  • Marriott lists the resort about 18 miles from the property to the airport.
  • Driving time is roughly 28 minutes in normal conditions.

By train

  • You noted Chicago Union Station as the main rail arrival point for some guests.
  • The resort also lists nearby Metra access points including Prairie View and Glenview stations.
  • Guests arriving by train should expect to use rideshare or taxi for the last segment to the resort.

Local transport

  • The hotel does not provide a standard airport shuttle.
  • Rideshare is the simplest option from ORD to Lincolnshire.
  • For guests making a longer weekend of it, having a car can make suburban travel easier.
Weekend

Things to do

Golf is the headline activity at the resort, and there are a few easy nearby options if you are staying for more than the ceremony day.

At the resort

  • Golf at Crane’s Landing Golf Club, with green fees waived for wedding guests as noted by the couple.
  • Enjoy the spa, indoor or outdoor pool, fitness center, restaurants, and walking areas around the property.
  • Catch a show at the Marriott Theatre, which is part of the resort campus.

Nearby ideas

  • Explore Hawthorn Mall for shopping, dining, and casual hangouts.
  • Visit Deerpath Park or the Des Plaines River Trail for a relaxed walk or bike ride.
  • For families or groups, Dave & Buster’s is another easy outing nearby.

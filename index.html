Travel

Getting there

For most out-of-town guests, O’Hare is the closest airport. The resort does not provide standard shuttle service, so plan on rideshare, taxi, rental car, or a pre-arranged pickup.

By air

  • Nearest airport: Chicago O’Hare International Airport (ORD).
  • The resort is about 18 miles from O’Hare.
  • Typical driving time is about 28 to 29 minutes depending on traffic.

By train

  • Chicago Union Station is your noted main train arrival point.
  • The resort also lists nearby Metra access such as Prairie View and Glenview.
  • Guests arriving by rail should plan the final segment by rideshare or taxi.

Local transport

  • The hotel does not provide regular airport shuttle service.
  • Rideshare is usually the simplest option from ORD.
  • If you are staying through multiple events, a car may be useful for suburban travel.
